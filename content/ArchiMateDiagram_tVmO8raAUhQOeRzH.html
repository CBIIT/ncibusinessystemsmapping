  IDRB : Application Component

IMPAC II Reporting database(IDRB) - is designed for querying and reporting as a decision support system. Users have no capability to change the data. IRDB will load data from the OLTP (On-Line Transaction and Processing) database which has no constraints and few foreign keys. The database design is de-normalized to facilitate data retrieval based on user query requests.

  eRA : Application Component

eRA(eRA) – Serves Application and the Extramural Research community. IT infrastructure to manage the receipt, processing ,review Award and Monitoring of research and non-research Grant. Supports the full life cycle of Grant administration. Also support other gov agency. eRA systems, including eRA Commons, ASSIST and IMPAC II modules. eRA integrates systems, including eRA Commons, ASSIST and IMPAC II modules
