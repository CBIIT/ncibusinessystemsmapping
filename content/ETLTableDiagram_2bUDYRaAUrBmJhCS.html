Define Requirements

Evaluate the bids on their ability to meet the requirements as stated in the RFP. It does NOT include an evaluation of price

Evaluate for Best Value

Evaluate bids for the best value compares the price to the ability to meet RFP requirements

Review Bidders' Past Performance

Review how the vendor has met similar requirements on other contracts in the past

Revise Final Proposal

Make any changes to the final RFP bid

Vendor
Determine Best Value

Determine the best value by comparing the price and ability to meet RFP requirements of all bids and selecting the one with the best value

Conduct Responsibility and Legal Reviews

Conduct reviews to ensure that the acquisition follows all laws, regulations, rules, processes and procedures

Notify and Debrief Unsuccessful Offerors

Notify the bidders that did not win the contract and debrief them on why they were not selected
