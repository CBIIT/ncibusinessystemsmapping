Model Elements
Name Description
  Contracting Officer’s Representative (COR) : Business Actor

The person in the program office who is responsible for directing the day-to-day work under a contract

  Vendor : Business Actor

Other entities that bid on contracts, provide responses to RFIs, win contracts, and provide services and products as defined in the contract

  Evaluate Contractor : Business Role

Evaluate the contractor's performance in providing the products/services specified in the contract

  Contracting Officer (CO) : Business Actor

The person in the Office of Acquisition who is responsible for all aspects of a contract from bid to execution, performance, and close-down of the contract

  Close Contract : Business Role

Perform all necessary steps to close out a contract, either at the end of the Period of Performance, or at termination of the contract

  Deliver Deliverables : Business Role

Deliver the artifacts, products and/pr services as specified in the contract

  Evaluate Deliverables : Business Role

Gather and evaluate all vendor deliverables against the agreed-upon acceptance criteria

  Deliver Requirements : Business Role

Deliver the services and products as specified on the contract

  Execute Contract : Business Role

Sign the contract and perform all necessary steps to make it legally binding

  Contract Closeout : Grouping
  Notify and Debrief Unsuccessful Offerors : Business Role

Notify the bidders that did not win the contract and debrief them on why they were not selected

  Award the Contract : Grouping
