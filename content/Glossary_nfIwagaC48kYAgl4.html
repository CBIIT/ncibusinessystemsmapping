Description

The organization or individual awarded a Grant or Cooperative Agreement by the NCI that assumes legal, financial, and scientific responsibility and accountability for both the awarded funds and the performance of the Grant-supported activity. A Recipient organization can be public or private, nonprofit, non-for-profit, or an educational institution, hospital, corporation, domestic or foreign agency, or other legally accountable entity.
