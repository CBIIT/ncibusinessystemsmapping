  eGrants : Application Component

NIH CSR USAGE NOTE- eGrants – The NCI Official Grant File, used to house all NCI internal file documentation, correspondence, and work products pertaining to Grant Award administration and Monitoring. All documents are stored for the life of the Grant until the records management policies are engaged. Interchanges data with the Datamart (I2E) and IMPAC II, but remains independent of the CBIIT umbrella.

 

OGA USAGE NOTE- OGA holds all of the Grant records in eGrants for 10 years and then destroys them.

  eRA commons : Application Component

eRA Commons - is an online interface where signing officials, principal investigators, trainees and post-DOC at institutes /organizations can access and share administrative information relating to research Grant. Can track the status of gran Application through submission process, view Summary Statement anscore letters, view notice oof Award, submit just in time information requested by the grantor agency, Submit the required DOC, financial ect, Submit a no cost extension Notification , submit annual progress RePORT electronically, Look for no cometing Grant progress RePORT instructions

 

PI/Application USAGE NOTE- eRA Commons – Grant applicants now have access to their Application scores and Summary Statement.
