 CAN Creation Process Diagram : Business Process Diagram

 

  1. Tu (OBF Budget Analyst) receives an NCI CAN Requests from an NCI Staff via the NCICANReuest@mail.NIH.gov mailbox 

  1. CAN requester fills out the NCI CAN Request Excel Sheet that can be found on the OBF-NCiConnect page  

  1. Tu performs a quality control check of the NCI CAN Request Excel Sheet data with the APIs Excel Sheet 

  1. If data is correct, move to the next step 

  1. If data is incorrect, Tu takes the following STEPS: 

  1. Reconfirms with CAN requester the data they submitted 

  1. Ask the CAN requester for a justification of why they are creating a new API, or using a different Division, Offices, and Center (DOC) for their CAN 

  1. Perform a consultation with Weston Ricks (OBF Director) on determining the best solution  

  1. After Weston’s approval to more forward, Tu creates a new API Line Operating via NBS  

  1. After all data on the NCI CAN Request Excel Sheet is correct, Tu processes the new CAN request in NBS 

  1. Logs into NBS  

  1. Finds a certain template on NBS for new CAN requests  

  1. Enters data on the template and submits the request
