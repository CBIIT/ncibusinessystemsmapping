  xTract : Application Component

xTRACT(xTRACT) – Allows Application, Recipient and assistants to create research training tables for progress reports and institutional training Grant Application. Initially, xTRACT supports the following training Grant Award mechanisms: T#@, TL1, T90/R90, and T15 for both progress reports and Grant Application.

 

System link - https://eRA.NIH.gov/services_for_applicants/other/xTRACT.cfm

  eRA : Application Component

eRA(eRA) – Serves Application and the Extramural Research community. IT infrastructure to manage the receipt, processing ,review Award and Monitoring of research and non-research Grant. Supports the full life cycle of Grant administration. Also support other gov agency. eRA systems, including eRA Commons, ASSIST and IMPAC II modules. eRA integrates systems, including eRA Commons, ASSIST and IMPAC II modules

 

User guides DOC - https://eRA.NIH.gov/modules_user-guides_documentation.cfm
