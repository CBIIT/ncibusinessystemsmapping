Evaluate bids for the best value compares the price to the ability to meet RFP requirements

Evaluate the bids on their ability to meet the requirements as stated in the RFP. It does NOT include an evaluation of price

  Answer Questions/Clarifications : Business Role

Respond to the RFP questions

  Request Clarifications/Corrections : Business Role

Request clarifications to any ambiguous or unclear items on the RFP

  Select and Prepare Source Selection Team : Business Role

Select the team that will evaluate the RFP bids, and give them instructions on how to evaluate, evaluation criteria, etc.
