Model Elements
Name Description
  Federal Program Manager : Business Actor

The Federal employee responsible for all aspects of a program. A program can consist of one or multiple projects

  Contracting Officer (CO) : Business Actor

The person in the Office of Acquisition who is responsible for all aspects of a contract from bid to execution, performance, and close-down of the contract. Provides acquisition advice. Determines source selection.

  Contract Specialist(CS) : Business Actor

Handle day-to-day work with acquisitions, similar function as the CO, but do not have the signing authority

  GSA eBuy : Business Actor

GSA eBuy acquisition tool to receive quotes electronically for products and services. https://www.ebuy.gsa.gov/ebuy/  

  GSA Advantage : Business Actor

GSA Advantage Online Shopping for products and services. https://www.gsaadvantage.gov/advantage/main/start_page.do

  GSA Acquisition Gateway : Business Actor

Market Research Tool for Federal Buyers and CS/CO – GSA Acquisition Gateway.  Includes a wealth of information. https://hallways.cap.gsa.gov/app/#/

  Subject Matter Expert : Business Actor

Person who has the expertise in the specific area(s) included in the contract

  Conduct Market Research : Business Role

Researching the market for similar services or products, different ways of meeting the requirements, what companies can deliver these, etc.
