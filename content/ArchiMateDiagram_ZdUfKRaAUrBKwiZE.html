Model Elements
Name Description
  Division Director : Business Actor

The Director of a Division

  Branch Chiefs (OA and Program) : Business Actor

The manager of a branch within an NCI division

  Contract Specialist(CS) : Business Actor

Handle day-to-day work with acquisitions, similar function as the CO, but do not have the signing authority

  Federal Program Manager : Business Actor

The Federal employee responsible for all aspects of a program. A program can consist of one or multiple projects

  Finance/Budget Officer : Business Actor

Reviews and provides approval of the use of fund for a procurement/contract

  Contracting Officer (CO) : Business Actor

The person in the Office of Acquisition who is responsible for all aspects of a contract from bid to execution, performance, and close-down of the contract. Provides acquisition advice. Determines source selection.

  Acquisitions Officer : Business Actor

Advises and assists agency officials to ensure that the mission of the  agency is achieved through the management of the agency’s acquisition activities.

  Determine Acquisition Strategy : Business Role

Determine the strategy for the acquisition, such as what contract vehicle to use, small business strategy, etc.
