Description

The NCI OA awards and administers contracts and simplified acquisitions in support of the institute’s mission to prevent, diagnose and treat cancer. During the course of the acquisition process, the OA will ensure that customer service is paramount, communications are open and continuous, positive relationships are fostered, sound business judgment is employed, and acquisition strategies are creative and proactive. In SO doing, the resulting Contract awards will wholly and completely meet CAA office needs, be compliant with Law and Regulation, and have a positive, long-lasting effect towards fulfilling the NCI’s mission to prevent, diagnose and treat cancer afflicting people worldwide.

The Frederick National Laboratory for Cancer Research (FNLCR) is supported by the Management and Operations Support Branch (MOSB) in the OA. If you are interested in learning more about the FNLCR, please visit the FNLCR Acquisition Portal.
