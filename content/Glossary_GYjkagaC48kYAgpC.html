Term - Unobligated Balance link

Properties
Name Value
Description

Funds not used by the completion of a grant’s project period. Grantees must report unobligated balances over 25 percent of total costs to the grants management specialist. Grants subject to SNAP may carry over unobligated funds from one budget period to another without prior approval, as stated in the Notice of Award.
