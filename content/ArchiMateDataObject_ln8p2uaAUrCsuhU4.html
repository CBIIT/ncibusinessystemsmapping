CBIIT USAGE NOTE - Datamart - I2E Reporting Database that retrieves data from NIH IMPAC II and NCI I2E Database. Datamart is a performance-tuned reporting database designed to give the NCI Extramural user community access to NCI-specific IMPAC and I2E enterprise information for querying, comprehensive analysis, and reporting across Extramural areas.
