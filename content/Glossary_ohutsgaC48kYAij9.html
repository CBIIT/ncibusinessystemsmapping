QVR (QVR) - IMPAC II tool that lets NIH staff search, view and retrieve detailed information about Grant Application and awards.

 

 

  • Provides a series of preformatted reports for most business areas - Receipt and Referral, Peer Review, Grant Management, Scientific Programs, Budget, Training, Portfolio Analysis, and other areas.
  • Acts as a portal for viewing a variety of research and research training Application and awards from IMPAC II, including:
    • Current data (data since 2007)
    • Historical data (back to 1970)
    • FY-end (FY) frozen records (called Pub files) (starting in FY 2002)
    • NIH Annual Intramural Project reports (from the last closed FY and before)
    • Grant-related publications from PubMed, via SPIRES database
  • • Finds scientifically similar projects related to one or more targeted Grant or manually entered text (such as from a publication or PA) using the various LIKE functionalities in QVR, or querying the RCDC Categorization data.
  • Searches and downloads information on people involved with IMPACII Grant as investigators, Key Personnel, participants, or reviewers.
  • Searches and downloads data on sites and collaborators in foreign countries as tracked using the Foreign Application and Component Tracking System (FACTS)
  • Allows access to broader extramural obligation data from the NIH Data Warehouse for Grant, contracts, interagency agreements, taps, etc.
