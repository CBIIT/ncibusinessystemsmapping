The Referral Application also manages requisitions and communications between stakeholders.

 

The typical workflow consists of searching by specific parameters, retrieve a hitlist of matching Application which gets reserved for processing, Application have to be released before another user can be processed by another user, although override is possible too.

 

Application roles are comprised entirely of CSR roles

 

Note: The IC Referral potentially corresponds to two separate activities:

 

  1. .Consideration for Funding
  2. R29 Level Peer Review

 

For NIH wide FOA the R29-Level Peer Review is typically coordinated by NIH CSR. For NCI originating FOA DEA is coordinating R29-Level Peer Review.

  : Callout

CSR (CSR) reviews the Application for completeness, determines the R15 of research and assigns the Application to an Institute or Center (IC) for review and funding consideration based on the focus and mission of the IC.

 

The Application is then assigned to either a SRG (SRG) within CSR or to the NCI DEA (DEA) for review.

 

When an Application is assigned to NCI, a Referral officer will examine and direct it to the appropriate NCI PO (see NCI DEA Referral Diagram).
