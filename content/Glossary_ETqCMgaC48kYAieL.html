Description

Paylist is an I2E Application module that allows selection of Grant to be paid, Coding of Grant for POR and Basic or Applied science and allows CAA staff to indicate which competing Grant Application should be paid.

CAA tool used to document paying Grant in rank order up to the payline.

 

Paylist is a module that allows NCI Extramural CAA staff to view the Paylist that is created via the Paylist Manager module and select the Application either for pay or skip. The CAA staff will provide the requested NCI dollar amount to support the Application that are selected for pay, which in accordance to the NCI Policy will be electronically routed to the CAA, Division, Funds, and Grant Management approvals. It also supports Coding of selected Grant within a Paylist.

 

Core Business Functions:

  • Supports electronic workflow that allows the NCI Extramural CAA staff to complete the selection of competing Grant in a Paylist for pay and/or skip. The saved Paylist will be electronically routed to the CAA, Division, Funds, and Grant Management approvals.
  • Provides the ability to assign Grant listed in a Paylist to a different Paylist.
  • Coding Paylists or Individual Grant Coding: Allows Coding of individual Grant in a Paylist (OR) en masse Coding of paylists for POR (POR) and Basic or Applied science
  • Built-in search capabilities: Performs default search based on the logged-in user’s role
  • RePORT Generation: Generates PDF or Excel RePORT on the search results
  • Adds the Official paycode to the system.

 

Primary Users:

  • Save/Complete Paylist: CAA Directors and Other CAA staff
  • CAA Approval: Branch Chief, Acting Branch Chief, & Division Level Approvers
  • Division Approval: Division Director, Acting Division Director, and Division Paylist Approver Delegate
  • Funds Certifier (OEFIA)
  • Grant Management Approver (OGA)

 

Size of Customer Base:

  • NCI CAA Staff
    • NCI CAA Directors – 300
    • Other CAA Staff – 130
  • CAA level Approver - Chief/Acting Chief- 65
  • Division Level Approver:
    • Division Director/Acting Division Director - 12
    • Division Paylist Approver - 12
  • Funds Certifier (ARC & OEFIA) - 13
  • Grant Management Approver (OGA) - 10
