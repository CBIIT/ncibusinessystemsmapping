  : Generic Connector
 FAM – Pulls data from I2E, IMPAC II, and NBS to monitor and report on Grant funding. FAM is a grant tracking system with multiple modules and functionalities for the sole purpose of tracking NCI grant awards each year. The information contained in FAM is also used for financial reporting to the OBF as well as senior level staff within NCI and NIH. FAM provides financial and other pertinent information for OEFIA staff to analyze financial data for all budgetary exercises. The data in FAM is presented as a sub-system or an extension database of the IMPAC II system. FAM is the primary tool used only by OEFIA staff to monitor and execute the budget for each fiscal year. At the end of a fiscal year once the data has been reconciled against other NCI systems the prior year data is available and the new fiscal year data is generated. : Data Store
