 DEA RAEB uses FLARE to apply Common Scientific Outline (CSO) codes, Basic Applied (BA) codes for budget reporting (also added by PDs – only added if PDs don’t add it), AIDS research codes (reconcile codes with OHAM at the end of the year), Women’s Health research codes, and Human Fetal Tissue Research codes. RAEB staff also performs a quality assurance process at this stage. Newly awarded grants are downloaded into FLARE every two weeks from IMPAC II for the DEA coding group to access and SIC and SITE data are uploaded to I2E quarterly. : Sub-Process
