  FAM : Application Component

OEFIA USAGE NOTE- FAM – Used to perform the budgetary exercises necessary to execute the extramural Grant Budget and to preform past trends and future financial analysis and modeling.

 

OEFIA USAGE NOTE 2- FAM – Pulls data from I2E, IMPAC II, and NBS to monitor and RePORT on Grant funding. FAM is a Grant tracking system with multiple modules and functionalities for the sole purpose of tracking NCI Grant awards each year. The information contained in FAM is also used for financial reporting to the OBF as well as senior level staff within NCI and NIH. FAM provides financial and other pertinent information for OEFIA staff to analyze financial data for all budgetary exercises. The data in FAM is presented as a sub-system or an extension database of the IMPAC II system. FAM is the primary tool used only by OEFIA staff to monitor and execute the Budget for each FY. At the end of a FY once the data has been reconciled against other NCI systems the prior year data is available and the new FY data is generated.

  eGrants : Application Component

NIH CSR USAGE NOTE- eGrants – The NCI Official Grant File, used to house all NCI internal file documentation, correspondence, and work products pertaining to Grant Award administration and Monitoring. All documents are stored for the life of the Grant until the records management policies are engaged. Interchanges data with the Datamart (I2E) and IMPAC II, but remains independent of the CBIIT umbrella.

 

OGA USAGE NOTE- OGA holds all of the Grant records in eGrants for 10 years and then destroys them.
