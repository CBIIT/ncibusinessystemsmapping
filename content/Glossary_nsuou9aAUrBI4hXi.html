Description

Implement state of the art portfolio and project management that enables CBIIT to:

- Govern it's portfolio while meeting the federal mandates (CPIC, FITARA, EPLC) and respond quickly to HHS data calls

- Conduct strategic as well as annual long term financial planning around it's portfolio

- Maintain alignment between it's portfolio of work and strategic objectives

- Evaluate the progress of it's work, manage dependencies, financials, risks, schedules and changes effectively

- Report and communicate multi-level management dashboards to enable CBIIT manage the goals and performance of it's Portfolio
