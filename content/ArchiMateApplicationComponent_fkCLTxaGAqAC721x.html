Description

eRA Commons - is an online interface where signing officials, principal investigators, trainees and post-DOC at institutes /organizations can access and share administrative information relating to research Grant. Can track the Status of gran Application through submission process, view Summary Statement anscore letters, view notice oof Award, submit JIT information requested by the grantor agency, Submit the required DOC, financial ect, Submit a no cost extension Notification , submit annual progress RePORT electronically, Look for no cometing Grant progress RePORT instructions

 

PI/Application USAGE NOTE- eRA Commons – Grant applicants now have access to their Application scores and Summary Statement.
