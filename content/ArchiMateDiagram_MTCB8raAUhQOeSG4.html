  RPPR : Application Component

RPPR(RPPR) – Reports are required annually to document Recipient accomplishments and compliance with therms of aard. They describe scientific progress, identify significant changes, RePORT on personnel, and describe plans for the subsequent Budget Period or year. The OM and Budget (OMB) has mandated that federal agencies implement a federal-wide RPPR (RPPR) for submission of required annual or other interim performance reporting on Grant and Cooperative Agreement awards to standardize Recipient reporting on federally –funded research projects.

  eRA : Application Component

eRA(eRA) – Serves Application and the Extramural Research community. IT infrastructure to manage the receipt, processing ,review Award and Monitoring of research and non-research Grant. Supports the full life cycle of Grant administration. Also support other gov agency. eRA systems, including eRA Commons, ASSIST and IMPAC II modules. eRA integrates systems, including eRA Commons, ASSIST and IMPAC II modules
