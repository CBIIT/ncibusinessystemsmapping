  IAR : Application Component

IAR(IAR) – Web-based system used in tandem wit the Peer Review module to expedite the Peer Review of Grant Application. It provides a standard process for reviewers to submit critiques, preliminary scores and final scores and to view Grant Application and related meeting materials via the eRA Commons. The IAR module is used during the pre-Award phase of the Application process. IAR allows one-click email capability between the SRO and reviewer.

 

System Link - https://eRA.NIH.gov/NIH_and_grantor_agencies/review_and_decision_making/internet_assisted_review.cfmResearch

  eRA : Application Component

eRA(eRA) – Serves Application and the Extramural Research community. IT infrastructure to manage the receipt, processing ,review Award and Monitoring of research and non-research Grant. Supports the full life cycle of Grant administration. Also support other gov agency. eRA systems, including eRA Commons, ASSIST and IMPAC II modules. eRA integrates systems, including eRA Commons, ASSIST and IMPAC II modules
