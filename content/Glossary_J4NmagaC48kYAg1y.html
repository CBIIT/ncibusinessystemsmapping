The Division of Cancer Control and Population Sciences both generates new knowledge and seeks to ensure that the products of cancer control research are effectively applied in all segments of the population. Through innovative research initiatives, leadership, and the synthesis of knowledge and its dissemination, we are building this program to be the nation's model for cancer control science.

 

https://cancercontrol.cancer.gov/od/index.html 

 

DCCPS has developed a set of Division-level scientific codes to characterize their awards. Those codes are entered through a DCCPS-developed tool, the Portfolio Management Assistant (PMA), which is also used for identifying, analyzing, and displaying award portfolios by code categories. A public-facing version of the DCCPS portfolio can be viewed at http://cancercontrol.cancer.gov.
