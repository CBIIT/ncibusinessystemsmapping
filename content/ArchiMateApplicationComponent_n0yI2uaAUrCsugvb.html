Description

NCI DEA USAGE NOTE - BSA Reports – Pulls information from CATS, I2E, and IMPAC II. The Status for all concepts presented at the BSA meeting is reflected in BSA Reports. BSA Reports provides FOA information for each concept through the years. Helps with Extramural Research Budget and financial planning. It reports all of the cleared concepts and the funded Grant listing.
