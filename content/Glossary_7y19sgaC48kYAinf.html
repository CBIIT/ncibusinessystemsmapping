Description

A publicly available document by which a Federal Agency makes known its intentions to Award discretionary Grant or cooperative agreements, usually as a result of competition for funds. Funding opportunity announcements may be known as CAA announcements, requests for Application, notices of funding availability, solicitations, or other names depending on the Agency and type of CAA. Funding opportunity announcements can be found at: https://Grant.NIH.gov/funding/ 

FOA Types

  1. Parent Announcements – Investigator Initiated
  2. IC Specific CAA Announcements (PA) – Broad Scientific Need
  3. Requests for Application (RFA) – Specific Research Needed in a Well Defined Scientific R15
