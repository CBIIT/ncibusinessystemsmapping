Data Store - NCI Funded Research Portfolio (NFRP) – Reports on NCI projects and NCI scientific coding and is managed by CSSI/KMSP. NFRP is considered Official Public Data by NCI, because it is vetted by the NCI Budget Office. Primary groups that supply data to NFRP are: RAEB (Extramural Grants and Research Contracts); DCEG (Intramural projects); CCR (Intramural projects); and, OBF (RMS and Leidos projects). link
