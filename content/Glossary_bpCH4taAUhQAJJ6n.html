Description

 The DGS System is a web-based application for producing RFPs and Contract documents. The system manages and implements the latest Federal Acquisition Regulations (FAR), Health and Human Services Acquisitions Regulations (HHSAR), and the NIH specific polices into these documents. Template/Work-form documents produced by the application will be available to all National Institutes of Health (NIH) acquisition offices and their users.
