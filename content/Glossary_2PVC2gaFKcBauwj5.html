The DEAIS ( DEAIS) contains tools and Application that support the business processes involved with the Referral and review of Grant Application and Contract proposals, concept tracking and reporting, support for BSA concept review and support for the NCAB second line of review. DEAIS incorporates eleven sub-systems relevant to this analysis including: CATS (concept development process, BSA Web (current concepts for BSA review), BSA Reports (current BSA review reports), BSA Archives (repository for BSA Reports), FOA Edit (FOA (RFA and PA) management), IRG Reports (IRG (IRG) recommendations), IRG Archives (repository for IRG recommendations), FOA (RFA and PA) Reports (FOA reporting tool), DEA Reporting System (FOA, Application & funding data reporting tool), DEA Blog (summaries and success rates), Program Coding (Research Coding at DOC/CAA level).
