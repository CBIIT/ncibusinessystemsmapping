NCI Funded Research Portfolio, a web application designed to find NCI funded research grants, contract awards, and intramural research projects.

 

https://fundedresearch.cancer.gov/nciportfolio/ 

 

NFRP8 is managed by CSSI/KMSP and reports on NCI projects and NCI scientific coding. NFRP data are considered by the NCI as Official Public Data. Primary groups that supply data to NFRP are: RAEB (Extramural Grants and Research Contracts); DCEG (Intramural projects); CCR (Intramural projects); and, OBF (RMS and Leidos projects).

At the end of each fiscal year, the Data Collection Process for NFRP is as follows:

 

  1. RAEB images final FY data and transfers it to an NFRP schema in I2E.
  2. KMSP accesses that data and runs scripts to load/copy it into the NFRP database.
  3. OBF collects data from Intramural groups and sends it via spreadsheets to KMSP.
  4. KMSP runs scripts to load the Intramural data to the NFRP database.
  5. OBF sends RMS and Leidos data to KMSP via spreadsheets.
  6. KMSP runs scripts to load the RMS and Leidos data to the NFRP database.

 

KMSP and OBF work closely during each new Fiscal Year’s Data Validation Process. KMSP performs the first level QA-QC of NFRP data as it is being loaded to the NFRP database. OBF validates and approves project and disease category data before that data is made public on NFRP. The total NCI budget is validated by OBF as the ‘Cancer’ category on NFRP. During the validation process, OBF staff have access to the NFRP development system where data are loaded as they are received. They can use the development system as an automated aid in their validation process. In addition, KMSP provides reports of the working FY data for OBF to validate.
