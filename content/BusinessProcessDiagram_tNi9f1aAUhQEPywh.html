  FAM – Pulls data from I2E, IMPAC II, and NBS to monitor and report on Grant funding. FAM is a grant tracking system with multiple modules and functionalities for the sole purpose of tracking NCI grant awards each year. The information contained in FAM is also used for financial reporting to the OBF as well as senior level staff within NCI and NIH. FAM provides financial and other pertinent information for OEFIA staff to analyze financial data for all budgetary exercises. The data in FAM is presented as a sub-system or an extension database of the IMPAC II system. FAM is the primary tool used only by OEFIA staff to monitor and execute the budget for each fiscal year. At the end of a fiscal year once the data has been reconciled against other NCI systems the prior year data is available and the new fiscal year data is generated. : Data Store
  IRG Reports – Downloads all extramural data from eRA, NCI specific data from I2E, NCAB data, and reports the NCAB Black Book and IRG Archives, which is a summary of all applications and NCAB actions. : Data Store
  IMPAC II Reporting Database (IRDB) – Provides grant data for reporting, running queries, and has data for local IC specific systems. : Data Store
  Office of Aids Research (OAR) Budget Reports. Premilimarny budget 2 years ahead of time, planning process. What do we plan on funding two years ahead of time. : Data Store
  IRDB : Sub-Process
  DataMart : Sub-Process
  DataMart - I2E Reporting Database that retrieves data from NIH IMPAC II and NCI I2E Database. DataMart is a performance-tuned reporting database designed to give the NCI Extramural user community access to NCI-specific IMPAC and I2E enterprise information for querying, comprehensive analysis, and reporting across Extramural areas. : Data Store
  BSA Reports – Pulls information from CATS, I2E, and IMPAC II. The status for all concepts presented at the BSA meeting is reflected in BSA Reports. BSA Reports provides FOA information for each concept through the years. Helps with extramural research budget and financial planning. It reports all of the cleared concepts and the funded grant listing. : Data Store
  Global Cancer Project Maps – Used by NCI Center for Global Health (CGH) to report international caner research, cancer care, and cancer outreach. Will be integrated with ICRP in the future. : Data Store
  PMA - Pulls grant data from IMPAC II and scientific coding from DataMart. DCCPS owns and manages the system. PMA not only displays portfolio information, it also enables the addition of DCCPS-specific coding in order to broaden the scope of coding terms associated with research funded by this NCI division. PMA also includes information on international components of DCCPS awards. : Data Store
  DEA Blog (DEA) – Presents tabular and graphical summaries of percentile information on select applications/awards (R01, R21), and success rates. It accepts comment posting. : Data Store
  NIDB – Reports on all NIH intramural projects, including NCI projects. : Data Store
  RePORT – Reports on all NIH projects, including NCI awards. Uses NIH-wide categorization through RCDC rather than NCI-wide coding for NCI Projects. : Data Store
  ICRP – Reports on cancer research funded by 110 funding agencies around the world. CRS manages the International Cancer Research Partnership (ICRP) data system. Currently RAEB provides data to ICRP, but plans have been made to have CSSI/KMSPB provide NFRP data to ICRP. : Data Store
