CSR manages the review of:

  • Research Grant (R-type) Application
  • Fellowship Application
  • SBIR (SBIR) and Technology Transfer (STTR) Grant Application (not Contract proposals)
  • Special CAA Announcements (PA), e.g.,
    • Academic Research Enhancement Award (R15),
    • NIH Director’s Pioneer Award CAA

 

NCI-DEA manages the review of:

  • PARs (PA with special receipt, Referral and/or review considerations) including CAA Projects, Cancer Center Support Grant, and Specialized Centers
  • RFA (Request for Application): Application received in response to NCI mission-specific initiatives
  • Training and Career Development Application
  • Contract proposals
