The Budget Fact Book provides a summary of the distribution of the FY Budget among the various NCI (NCI) research programs and funding mechanisms. NCI funding policies related to research Grant awards are also provided.

The Budget Fact Book is compiled and published by OBF.

 

  • NCI Budget Summary Data: This section provides detailed data about funds available to NCI and information on how NCI obligated its funding.
  • Extramural Programs: NCI uses most of its Budget to fund extramural Grant and contracts. This section provides information about extramural funding by Grant activity, institution, state, and country.
  • Historical Trends: This section contains information about the history of NCI appropriations and the Professional Judgment (Bypass) Budget, as well as data on funding trends and staffing levels.

The dollar amounts displayed in the Budget Fact Book represent direct appropriated funds only, unless otherwise denoted.
