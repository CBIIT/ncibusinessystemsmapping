  AMS : Application Component

Account Management System(AMS) – User-friendly module to create, manage and search existing accounts in eRA Common, iEdison, And ASSIST as well as eRA modules used by agency staff. Depending on role, users from Organizations., Universities, Federal Agencies, IC Coordinators, the State department (for FACTS) and the eRA service desk staff are able to access and use the functionality.

  ASSIST : Application Component

Application Submission System & Interface for Submission Tracking(ASSIST) - NIH’s online system for the preparation, submission and tracking of rant Application through Grants.gov to NIH.Not required may just use Grants.gov. Allows for collaboration, Pre-submission validation, Pre-population of data from eRA Commons, Pre-submission preview of Application in NIH format, Submission Status checking for both Grant. Gov and eRA Commons, ability to import sub Award Budget data from external sources, Ability to copy Application data from one announcement to another. Profiles

 

Owned by NIH

  eRA : Application Component

eRA(eRA) – Serves Application and the Extramural Research community. IT infrastructure to manage the receipt, processing ,review Award and Monitoring of research and non-research Grant. Supports the full life cycle of Grant administration. Also support other gov agency. eRA systems, including eRA Commons, ASSIST and IMPAC II modules. eRA integrates systems, including eRA Commons, ASSIST and IMPAC II modules
