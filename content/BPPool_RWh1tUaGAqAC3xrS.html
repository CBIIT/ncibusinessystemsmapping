ICRP's notion of International Research is different from (having a) Foreign Component as defined in FACTS.

For ICRP International collaboration means the Research Funding Sponsor and the Research Funding Recipient are located in different countries.

There are metadata that distinguish between countries where research is executed and where the funded institution is located.
