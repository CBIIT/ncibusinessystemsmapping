trials_using_interventions Table
Column Name Data Type PK / FK Nullable Description
month_date_key integer(0) No

month_date_key is a key into the dim_date dimension table. Although month_date_key is the first day of a particular month, the total... columns are summed through the entire MONTH.  For example when month_date_key=20180601, total_trials_opened_during_period is the number of trials opened during the entire month of June 2018.

trial_type_key smallint(0) No
study_source_key smallint(0) No
intervention_key integer(0) No
total_trials_opened_during_period integer(0) No

The total number of trials that OPENED during this month/year.  OPENED is defined the same way the DT4 report defines OPENED.

total_trials_closed_during_period integer(0) No

The total number of trials that CLOSED during this month/year.

total_trials_started_during_period integer(0) No

The total number of trials that STARTED on this date.  The start date is from aurora trial_date where date_type is either 'ACTUAL_START_DATE' or 'ANTICIPATED_START_DATE'.

running_total_trials_opened integer(0) No

The running total number of trials having this trial_type and this study_source, offering this intervention, that have opened since the beginning of time up to and including this period.  A trial may have more than 1 intervention.

running_total_trials_closed integer(0) No

The running total number of trials having this trial_type and this study_source, offering this intervention, that have closed since the beginning of time up to and including this period.  A trial may have more than 1 intervention.

running_total_trials_started integer(0) No

The running total number of trials having this trial_type and this study_source, offering this intervention, that have started since the beginning of time up to and including this period.  A trial may have more than 1 intervention.

total_accrual_during_period integer(0) No

The total number of patients accrued on all trials of this trial_type and study_source, using this intervention, during this period.

running_total_accrual integer(0) No

The running total number of patients accrued on trials having this trial_type and this study_source, offering this intervention, since the beginning of time up to and including this period.  A trial may have more than 1 intervention.
