IAR is an eRA Application module: A system outside of Peer Review which allows reviewers to submit critiques and preliminary scores for Application they are reviewing and allows Reviewers, SRO, and ESA to view all critiques in preparation for a meeting. IAR creates a preliminary Summary Statement body containing submitted critiques for the SRO or ESA.
