Awaiting Receipt of Application (ARA) is an application that supports NCI approval process for CSR unsolicited applications (required mostly for those requesting high funding).

I2E application

 

https://era.nih.gov/nih_and_grantor_agencies/other/awaiting_receipt_of_applications.cfm 

The main role of the Awaiting Receipt of Applications (ARA) module is to electronically track and link information received in advance of submission to a grant application, after the application is received or logged into the eRA system. For instance, an Institute/Center may be willing to accept a grant application that is at or over the $500K limit of direct costs in any year. The IC uses an ARA form to electronically inform CSR's Division of Receipt and Referral (DRR) of this concurrence. DRR staff will then match the ARA request from the IC to the right application and eRA will automatically inform the IC.

ARA also facilitates requests for assignments from ICs and other information that needs to be connected to a specific application.

The new ARA replaces the manual paper-based process that has been used to match information to applications.
