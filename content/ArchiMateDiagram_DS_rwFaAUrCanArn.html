Model Elements
Name Description
  OA Team Lead : Business Actor

A team lead in the Office of Acquisitions

  Contract Specialist(CS) : Business Actor

Handle day-to-day work with acquisitions, similar function as the CO, but do not have the signing authority

  Contracting Officer (CO) : Business Actor

The person in the Office of Acquisition who is responsible for all aspects of a contract from bid to execution, performance, and close-down of the contract. Provides acquisition advice. Determines source selection.

  Federal Program Manager : Business Actor

The Federal employee responsible for all aspects of a program. A program can consist of one or multiple projects

  Special Emphasis Panel (Peer Review) Chairperson : Business Actor
  Technical Evaluation Panel : Business Actor

Member of the team that evaluates the bid proposals

  Perform Technical Evaluations : Business Role

Evaluate the bids on their ability to meet the requirements as stated in the RFP. It does NOT include an evaluation of price
