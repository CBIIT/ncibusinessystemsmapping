Assignment Change Request (ACR) - IMPAC II system enables a change in a grant application original assignment after submission to be initiated, routed and approved electronically.

 

Allowable application assignment changes, involving use of the Assignment Change Request (ACR), include:

  • Change Council Date.
  • Change Application Number.
  • Withdraw or Return an Application.
  • Change Study Section (in certain circumstances)
    • To CA from another IC or CSR; or
    • From CA to another IC or CSR.
