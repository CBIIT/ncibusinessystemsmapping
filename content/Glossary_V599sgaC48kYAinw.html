Description

A formal statement that describes and gives notice to the grantee community of the existence of an NIH-wide or individual Institute/Center extramural research activity/interest
or announces the initiation of a new or modified activity/interest or mechanism of support and invites applications for grant or cooperative agreement support. PAs are published in the NIH Guide to Grants and Contracts: http://grants.nih.gov/grants/guide/index.html. Funds may or may not be set aside for PAs.

 

Program Announcement (PA or PAR)

  • Signals interest in important and/or understudied area of science to scientific community.

  • Generally involves multiple application receipt dates.

  • Generally uses only standard review criteria.

  • Has no money committed up-front.

 

NIH currently has 27 active Parent Program Announcements (PAs). Six Categories of Parent PAs:

  • Research (R) Announcements (R01; R03; R13; R15; R21; R41/R42; R43/R44);
  • Research Training (T) Announcements (T32; T35); Career Development (K) Announcements (K01; K02; K08; K23; K24; K25;
  • K99/R00); Fellowship (F) Announcements (F30 [x 2]; F31; F31-Diversity; F32; F33);
  • Administrative Supplements Announcement (for all activity codes; to promote diversity; to promote re-entry).
  • Post-award Administrative Action Announcements (Type 6; Type 7)
