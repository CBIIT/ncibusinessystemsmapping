Model Elements
Name Description
  HHS Senior Procurement Executive (HHS SPE) : Business Actor

The HHS approving official for determinations under Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) 17.104(b).

  NIH DAPE : Business Actor

Division of Acquisition Policy and Evaluation. CO needs to notify this group when they notify the HHS Congressional Liaison

  NIH HCA : Business Actor
  NCI OA Procurement Analyst : Business Actor
  NCI OA Contractor Analyst : Business Actor
  NCI OA Procurement Technician : Business Actor
  NCI FOIA Office : Business Actor

Reviews acquisition documentation post award to determine if sensitive information is included and therefore needs to be protected in FOIA requests

  Board of Scientific Advisors(BSA) : Business Actor

Makes recommendations on research priorities conducted or supported by the Institute. This includes the evaluation of NCI awarded grants, cooperative agreements and contracts and concept review of those activities which it considers meritorious and consistent with the Institute's programs

  Contracting Officer’s Representative (COR) : Business Actor

The person in the program office who is responsible for directing the day-to-day work under a contract

  Contracting Officer (CO) : Business Actor

The person in the Office of Acquisition who is responsible for all aspects of a contract from bid to execution, performance, and close-down of the contract. Provides acquisition advice. Determines source selection.

  Contract Specialist(CS) : Business Actor

Handle day-to-day work with acquisitions, similar function as the CO, but do not have the signing authority

  Collect Information for Data Call : Business Role

Collect all relevant information for a request for data. This could be internal or external to the organization
