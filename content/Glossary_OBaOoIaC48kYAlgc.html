Description All Grant Application submitted to the NIH go to the DRR (DRR) within the CSR (CSR). After receiving the Application DRR:
  • Checks the Application for completeness
  • Determines R15 of research and determines which specific NIH Institute or Center to assign it to for possible funding
  • Assigns an Application identification number
  • Assigns Application to a specific SRG, also known as a SRG (SRG) or review committee that has the expertise to evaluate the scientific and technical merit of the Application.
  • CSR coordinates the reviews for most R01s, fellowships, and Small Business Application, as well as some PA, PARs, & RFA's
  • Institutes and Centers coordinate the review for Application that have Institute-specific features such as CAA project Grant, training Grant, career development awards, and responses to Requests for Application.
