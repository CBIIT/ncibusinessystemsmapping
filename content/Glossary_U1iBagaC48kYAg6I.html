The OGA (OGA) supports Grant and cooperative agreements awarded to scientific institutions, small businesses and individuals.

 

OGA responsibilities are complex and critical to the NCI's mission and success. We plan and direct all NCI business-related activities associated with the negotiation, Award, and ongoing management of NCI Grant and cooperative agreements to help financially support cancer research activities throughout the United States and around the world. OGA staff ensures that all legal, regulatory, and policy requirements are met by NCI and the Recipient.
