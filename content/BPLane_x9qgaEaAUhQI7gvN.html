 NIH FACTS Account? : Gateway
 Create NIH FACTS Account : Task

In order to access the FACTS, the CO must request appropriate user role from their IC’s FACTS eRA coordinator. The CO will then go to https://public.eRA.NIH.gov/FACTS and login using the NIH Single-Sign-On (SSO) User Name and Password, or the Smart Card and Personal Identification Number (PIN). Before entering information on the foreign SITE(s), the CO will be able to review FACTS User Guides that are available at the following eRA Intranet website: http://inside.eRA.NIH.gov/FACTS/user_guide.cfm.

Basic information about the Grant:the Grant’s topic, summary, and the foreign institution(s) involved. The Grant proposal also includes the domestic researcher’s name, domestic Recipient institution, foreign Recipient institution, foreign and total funding, research objectives, and information on the use of animal and/or human subjects

 Approval? : Gateway

The Department’s role in reviewing NIH Grant proposals is two-fold. R29, the responsible officer, who may, as needed, include other sections at post in the review process, looks for any possible derogatory information on foreign institutions slated to receive NIH funding. Second, the responsible officer determines whether the NIH-funded work is on a politically-sensitive issue or population, and if SO, whether pursuing it could harm the United States’ bilateral relationship with the host government or hinder broader U.S. foreign policy objectives.
