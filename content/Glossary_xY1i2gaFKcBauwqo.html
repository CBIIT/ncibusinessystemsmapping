Description

The FAM system is used (by OEFIA) to perform the budgetary exercises necessary to execute the extramural budget. PFR and GPMATS are two I2E modules with data that also update FAM.

 

FAM maintains up-to-date estimates of NCI award disbursals and planned future spending.
