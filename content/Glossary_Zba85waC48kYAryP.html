Mission: OER provides the corporate framework for NIH research administration, ensuring scientific integrity, public accountability, and effective stewardship of the NIH extramural research portfolio.

They are responsible for publication and distribution of NCI authored FOAs via the NIH Guide to Grants and Contracts.

 

Offices within OER

  • Immediate Office of the Director (IMOD)
  • Office of Administrative Operations (OAO)
  • Office of Data Analysis Tools and Systems (DATA)
  • Office of Extramural Programs (OEP)
  • Office of Laboratory Animal Welfare (OLAW)
  • Office of Policy for Extramural Research Administration (OPERA)
  • Office of Research Information Services (ORIS)
  • Statistical Analysis and Reporting Branch (SARB)
