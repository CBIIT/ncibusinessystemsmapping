P01

  • Support for integrated, multi-project research projects involving a number of independent investigators who share knowledge and common resources
  • Each project contributes or is directly related to the common theme of the total research effort, thus forming a system of research activities and projects directed toward a well-defined research CAA goal
  • Specific dollar limit unless specified in FOA
