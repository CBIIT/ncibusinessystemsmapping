Model Elements
Name Description
  Approval Authority : Business Actor

The person on the program side who signs off on a procurement. This could be the DOC Director, a Branch Chief, etc.

  Regulatory Analyst : Business Actor

Assists in the regulatory review of the procurement

  Federal Project Manager : Business Actor

The Federal employee responsible for all aspects of a project

  Compliance Manager : Business Actor

Leads the work to ensure that the organization is meeting regulatory obligations such as safety and governance concerns

  Attorney/General Counsel : Business Actor

Leads the legal review of all aspects of the procurement. Handles legal challenges from vendors.

  Contracting Officer (CO) : Business Actor

The person in the Office of Acquisition who is responsible for all aspects of a contract from bid to execution, performance, and close-down of the contract

  Acquisitions Officer : Business Actor

Advises and assists agency officials to ensure that the mission of the  agency is achieved through the management of the agency’s acquisition activities.

  Finance/Budget Officer : Business Actor

Reviews and provides approval of the use of fund for a procurement/contract

  Market Research Lead : Business Actor

The person conducting the market research to determine what products/services are available, and potential sources

  Subject Matter Expert : Business Actor

Person who has the expertise in the specific area(s) included in the contract

  RFP Author : Business Actor

The person(s) writing the statement of work or performance work statement

  Federal Program Manager : Business Actor

The Federal employee responsible for all aspects of a program. A program can consist of one or multiple projects

  Contract Requestor : Business Actor

Leads the work to ensure that the organization is meeting regulatory obligations such as safety and governance concerns

  Create Source Selection Evaluation Strategy : Business Role

Determine the strategy for evaluating RFP bids

  Develop SSP : Business Role

Develop a plan for how the vendor bids will be evaluated, including evaluation criteria

  Develop RFP : Business Role

Write the Request for Proposal (RFP)

  Develop RFP and SSP : Grouping
  Obtain Funding Approval : Business Role

Obtain the necessary funds to pay for the services/products included in a contract

  Manage Business, Management, Technical, and Logistics Risks : Business Role

List, analyze and monitor the risks associated with an acquisition and the ensuing contract

  Determine Acquisition Strategy : Business Role

Determine the strategy for the acquisition, such as what contract vehicle to use, small business strategy, etc.

  Develop acquisition plan : Grouping
  Obtain Funding : Business Role

Obtaining the necessary funds to pay for the services/products included in the RFP over the full lifetime of the contract

  Conduct Market Research : Business Role

Researching the market for similar services or products, different ways of meeting the requirements, what companies can deliver these, etc.

  Define Requirements : Business Role

Define the requirements to be included in a Request for Proposal (RFP) and which a vendor will need to deliver and adhere to. This could include detailed services, products, etc.
