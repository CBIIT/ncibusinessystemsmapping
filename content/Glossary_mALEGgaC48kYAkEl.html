The CSSI (CSSI) plans, implements and oversees the NCI (NCI) strategic initiatives in advanced technologies, and foundational resources needed to enable and advance basic, translational and clinical cancer research across the various cancer research communities that comprise the NCI research portfolio. This KMSP (KMSP) represents NCI as the Institute Lead on National Institutes of Health (NIH) Scientific Health "Knowledge Management" reporting initiatives; and directs the scientific health data analysis functions. The KMSP Branch represents NCI in the trans-NIH Knowledge Management Initiatives and the primary goals of the Branch are to accomplish the knowledge management requirements of the NCI, NIH, and the HHS (DHHS) centralized extramural initiatives in scientific health analysis and data base management.
