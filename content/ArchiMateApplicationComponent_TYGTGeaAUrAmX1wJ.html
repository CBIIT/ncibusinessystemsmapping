*CSSI/KMSP USAGE NOTE- NFRP (NFRP) – Reports on NCI projects and NCI Research Coding and is managed by CSSI/KMSP. NFRP is considered Official Public Data by NCI, because it is vetted by the NCI Budget Office. Primary groups that supply data to NFRP are: RAEB (Extramural Grant and Research Contracts); DCEG (Intramural projects); CCR (Intramural projects); and, OBF (RMS and Leidos projects).
