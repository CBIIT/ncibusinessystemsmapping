Description

Within the Status module of the eRA Commons, users will find a feature to submit Just-In-Time information when requested by the NIH. NIH policy allows the submission of certain elements of a competing application to be deferred. Through this module, institutions can electronically submit the information that is requested after the review, but before award. JIT includes, certification of IRB approval, proof of human subjects education training, certification of IACUC approval, Other Support for key personnel, etc.
