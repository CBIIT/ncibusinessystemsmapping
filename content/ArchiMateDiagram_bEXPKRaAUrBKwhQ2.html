Model Elements
Name Description
  Finance/Budget Officer : Business Actor

Reviews and provides approval of the use of fund for a procurement/contract

  N/A : Business Actor
  Subject Matter Expert : Business Actor

Person who has the expertise in the specific area(s) included in the contract

  RFP Author : Business Actor

The person(s) writing the statement of work or performance work statement

  Federal Program Manager : Business Actor

The Federal employee responsible for all aspects of a program. A program can consist of one or multiple projects

  Contract Requestor : Business Actor

Leads the work to ensure that the organization is meeting regulatory obligations such as safety and governance concerns

  Obtain Funding : Business Role

Obtaining the necessary funds to pay for the services/products included in the RFP over the full lifetime of the contract

  Conduct Market Research : Business Role

Researching the market for similar services or products, different ways of meeting the requirements, what companies can deliver these, etc.

  Define Requirements : Business Role

Define the requirements to be included in a Request for Proposal (RFP) and which a vendor will need to deliver and adhere to. This could include detailed services, products, etc.
