Model Elements
Name Description
  OA Director : Business Actor

Advises and assists agency officials to ensure that the mission of the  agency is achieved through the management of the agency’s acquisition activities

  NCI OM : Business Actor
  NCI OD : Business Actor
  Division Director : Business Actor

The Director of a Division

  Program Manager's Immediate Supervisor : Business Actor

The person to whom the program manager reports

  OA Branch Chief : Business Actor

The manger of a branch within OA

  OA Team Lead : Business Actor

A team lead in the Office of Acquisitions

  Contracting Officer’s Representative (COR) : Business Actor

The person in the program office who is responsible for directing the day-to-day work under a contract

  Requiring Office : Business Actor

The office requesting the acquisition, write the SOW, IGCE/IGE, technical evaluation factors

  GSA eBuy : Business Actor

GSA eBuy acquisition tool to receive quotes electronically for products and services. https://www.ebuy.gsa.gov/ebuy/  

  GSA Advantage : Business Actor

GSA Advantage Online Shopping for products and services. https://www.gsaadvantage.gov/advantage/main/start_page.do

  GSA Acquisition Gateway : Business Actor

Market Research Tool for Federal Buyers and CS/CO – GSA Acquisition Gateway.  Includes a wealth of information. https://hallways.cap.gsa.gov/app/#/

  Contract Specialist (CS) : Business Actor

Handle day-to-day work with acquisitions, similar function as the CO, but do not have the signing authority

  Contracting Officer (CO) : Business Actor

The person in the Office of Acquisition who is responsible for all aspects of a contract from bid to execution, performance, and close-down of the contract. Provides acquisition advice. Determines source selection.

  Conduct Market Research : Business Role

Researching the market for similar services or products, different ways of meeting the requirements, what companies can deliver these, etc.

  Define Requirements : Business Role

Define the requirements to be included in a Request for Proposal (RFP) and which a vendor will need to deliver and adhere to. This could include detailed services, products, etc.

  Market Research Lead : Business Role

conducts the market research to determine what products/services are available, and potential sources

  Obtain Funding : Business Role

Obtaining the necessary funds to pay for the services/products included in the RFP over the full lifetime of the contract

  RFP Author : Business Role

The person conducting the market research to determine what products/services are available, and potential sources

  Create Solicitation : Business Function 		Could be creating written solicitation (RFQ, RFP, RFI) Could also be contacting vendors for verbal or informal written quote, etc.
  Finance/Budget Officer : Business Actor

Reviews and provides approval of the use of fund for a procurement/contract

  Subject Matter Expert : Business Actor

Person who has the expertise in the specific area(s) included in the contract

  Federal Program Manager : Business Actor

The Federal employee responsible for all aspects of a program. A program can consist of one or multiple projects

  Contract Requestor : Business Actor

Leads the work to ensure that the organization is meeting regulatory obligations such as safety and governance concerns
