versus

NCI IRG: Composed of FACA-chartered subcommittees; review of training and career development Application, and final Score for Cancer Center Support Grant Application (NCI only)

 

Initial review of NIH Grant Application is handled by the "Initial Review Groups" comprising both CSR (CSR) study sections and review committees of the individual NIH institutes.

Review activities of the CSR (CSR) are organized into Integrated Review Groups (IRG). Each IRG represents a cluster of study sections in a general scientific R15. Application generally are assigned R29 to an IRG, and then to a specific SRG within that IRG for evaluation of scientific merit.
