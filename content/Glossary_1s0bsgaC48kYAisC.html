Concept to Award Tracking System (CATS) is an application that follows the progress of concepts from creation to final approval.

Concept to Award Tracking System (CATS) is built and managed by NCI DEA. This system enables NCI staff to manage the development and approval of concepts as well as provide the status of current and previously approved concepts. Questions should be directed to DEA Application Support.
