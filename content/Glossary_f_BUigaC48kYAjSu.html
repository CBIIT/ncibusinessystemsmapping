Term - New Investigator link

Properties
Name Value
Description A PO/PI who has not previously competed successfully as a PO/PI for a substantial independent research Award is considered a New Investigator. For example, a PO/PI who has previously received a competing NIH R01 research Grant is no longer considered a New Investigator. However, a PO/PI who has received a Small Grant (R03) or an Exploratory/Developmental Research Grant Award (R21) retains his or her status as a New Investigator. A complete list of NIH Grant that do not disqualify a PO/PI from being considered a New Investigator can be found at http://Grant.NIH.gov/Grant/new_investigators/index.htm
